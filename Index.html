SCOTT HOLDINGS & CONSULTANTS BUSINESS PROPOSAL

The company was established in 2020 by the director and CEO Mr. Siphosethu Msane during the Covid-19 pandemic.
We saw an opportunity within the market as most employees were being retrenched that would then mean most individuals would then have to open their own companies to maintain their standard of living.
Thereafter the company expanded into the accounting field as our director obtained his degree in accounting and finance in 2020 and began his articles from October 2020 accredited by SAIPA.

About Us

What we do

We provide services which are essential financial and consulting services for any operating organization.
Our main focus when we started operating was to work closely with small businesses and closed corporations as that would provide a platform for both organizations to grow and move forward.
We pride ourselves in making sure that the services we provide ensure that the clients are aware of their financial standing and free from mismanaging their funds in order to minimize the risk of running the company at a loss. We mostly consult our clients.
